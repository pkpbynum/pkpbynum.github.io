Bio

Hello, my name is Peter Bynum. I am currently a freshman studying Computer Science at Harvard University. Along with coding, I have several other artistic and academic interests, such as ceramics, philosophy, and most of all, music. Since my start as a flautist in elementary school, classical music has grown to become my primary passion. I have spent many hours refining my skill. This site works as a waypoint to further discover that progress.
